YOGI RUN @ Gokarna on Jan 1 2019

We going to Gokarna and we can’t wait to take you to this magical land of daily offerings, temples, incredible food, sweet people, mystical mountains, vibrant jungles and more importantly the beach. The temple is one of the seven sacred Muktikshetras in Karnataka. Gokarna is magical it’s easy to see beyond the hype as soon as you arrive and feel the warmth of the land, the people and the culture. This Run is designed to open up your heart, body and soul to a life of offering and ritual of which you will experience every single day. We wanted to be in the Beach so that we could feel the pulse of the waves, the culture and be close to the water and the sacred space.
